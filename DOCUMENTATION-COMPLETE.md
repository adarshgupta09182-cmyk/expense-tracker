## 🔑 Key Information

### What's the Problem?
Your app is deployed on Vercel, but login/registration are failing because there's no database connected.

### What's the Solution?
Connect to Supabase (free PostgreSQL database) in about 20 minutes.

### What Do I Need?
1. Supabase account (free)
2. Connection string from Supabase
3. JWT_SECRET (generate new one)
4. 5 minutes to add to Vercel

### How Long Will It Take?
About 20-25 minutes total (including reading documentation).

3. **Generate a strong JWT_SECRET** - don't use simple strings
